About

Younes Belkada is a former student at Polytech Sorbonne, Sorbonne Université where he was enrolled in the Applied Mathematics and Computer Science Master’s program. He did his exchange semester at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne at the Data Science Master. Younes is now enrolled in the Mathematics, Machine Learning, Vision Master’s program at ENS Paris-Saclay."

During his curriculum, Younes worked for Chainos Solution in VietNam, ISIR in Paris, Arbrea Labs and VITA Lab in Switzerland and at Intel Corporation in Dublin, Ireland.

Younes' interests focuses on Deep Learning applied to exciting real-world problems. His main interests are :

  • Computer Vision
  • Human pose estimation
  • Unsupervised Learning
  • Autonomous driving
